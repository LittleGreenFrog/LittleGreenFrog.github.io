Movie Posters and Wallpapers

Movie and TV show posters make ideal iPhone wallpapers. The Movie Posters and Wallpapers app makes it easy to download the poster or scene from your favorite show and use it as your iPhone Lock or Home screen wallpaper.

Just choose your favorite Movie or TV show.

Swipe through all the posters.

And scenes. And save them to your iPhone photos. From there you can set them as your wallpaper as usual.
